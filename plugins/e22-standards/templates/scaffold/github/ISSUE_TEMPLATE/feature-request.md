## What do you want?

In one or two sentences, describe what you would like the product to do.

## Why?

What problem does this solve? What can the user do, or do better, after this is built?

## Who is it for?

Which users will use this? Existing users, new users, internal team, admins, or another group?

## Where in the app?

If this applies to a specific screen, page, or flow, mention it. Screenshots help.

## Design source or examples (optional)

If you explored this in **Claude Design**, paste the URL here — that's the canonical way to share a design with the dev team.

Other design sources work too: Figma links, screenshots, screen recordings, or examples from another product.

## How important is this?

- [ ] Critical — blocking users today
- [ ] High — significant improvement, want it soon
- [ ] Medium — would be nice to have
- [ ] Low — backlog
